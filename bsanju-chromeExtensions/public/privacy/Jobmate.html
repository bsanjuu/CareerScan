Privacy Policy for JOBMate for LinkedIn

Last updated: April 18, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in relation to the "JOBMate for LinkedIn" Chrome extension (the "Extension"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data or usage information through this Extension. The Extension operates entirely locally on your device.

3. What We Don't Do

  • We don't collect your personal information
  • We don't track your browsing activity
  • We don't store any data about your use of the Extension
  • We don't use cookies or any tracking technologies
  • We don't share data with third parties because we don't collect any

4. How the Extension Works

The Extension enhances LinkedIn pages by highlighting job posts and injecting reusable message templates. All functionality occurs locally in your browser:

  • Templates are stored using Chrome's local storage only
  • No data is transmitted to any external server
  • No personalization, tracking, or profiling is performed

5. Chrome Permissions

The Extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • "storage" - Used to store message templates locally
  • "tabs" - Required to access the current LinkedIn tab
  • "scripting" - Required to inject content scripts into LinkedIn pages
  • "activeTab" - Used to modify the content of the currently active tab
  • "https://*.linkedin.com/*" - Required to run functionality specifically on LinkedIn pages

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

7. Contact Us
